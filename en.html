Features





YouTube Music client with Material Design 3

Discover the ultimate YouTube Music experience on Android with our app, meticulously designed with the principles of Material Design 3. This app offers you a sleek, intuitive, and modern interface, optimized to provide an exceptional user experience and advanced functionality.

Modern and Attractive Design

The app stands out for its design based on Material Design 3, ensuring a visually harmonious interface with smooth transitions and an arrangement of elements that follows Google's latest design patterns. Vibrant colors and modern fonts are carefully selected to provide an aesthetic and attractive experience.

Intuitive and Customizable Interface

Easily navigate through a simplified interface that allows quick access to all YouTube Music features. Customize your experience by adjusting themes and display settings according to your preferences.

Explore and Discover Music

Access a vast music catalog, from the latest hits to your favorite classics. Use the advanced search function to quickly find songs, albums, and artists, with smart suggestions based on your preferences and listening habits.

High-Quality Playback

Enjoy music with exceptional sound quality, optimized for different devices and network conditions. The app supports various audio formats and quality settings to suit your needs.

Playlists and Downloads

Create, edit, and organize your playlists easily to always have your favorite music at hand. Download songs and entire albums for offline listening, ideal for times when you don't have an internet connection.

Full Integration with YouTube Music

Sync your YouTube Music account to access your music libraries, playlists, and personalized recommendations. Receive notifications about new releases, concerts, and events from your favorite artists.

Advanced Features

Control playback with voice commands, gestures, and on-screen controls for a hands-free and convenient experience. Take advantage of support for Chromecast and Bluetooth devices, allowing you to stream your music to a wide range of external devices.

Efficient Library Management

Organize and manage your music library with advanced filtering and categorization tools. Quickly access your recent songs, saved albums, and most popular playlists.

Multilingual Support

Enjoy the app in multiple languages, with support for accurate translations and culturally relevant localization.

Continuous Updates and Improvements

The app is regularly updated to incorporate new features, improve security, and optimize performance, always ensuring the best possible experience.

  • Play songs from YT/YT Music without ads
  • Background Playback
  • Search for songs, videos, albums, and playlists from YouTube Music
  • Library Management
  • Cache and download songs for offline playback
  • Synchronized Lyrics
  • Lyrics Translator (experimental)
  • Adjust Tempo/Pitch
  • Dynamic Theme
  • Login Support
